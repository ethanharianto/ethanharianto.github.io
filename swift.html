D'Andre Swift
Graphic Edit

The original picture depicted Swift after he scored a touchdown against the Los Angeles Rams.

I was inspired by some online posts of graphic designers editing football players into imaginary teams for their fantasy football leagues, and thought that I would give a shot at it. I found a picture of D'Andre Swift who scored the most points for my win in week one (narrowly beating out AJ Brown). I was always a fan of the New Orleans Saints' dark home jerseys and thought I would incorporate black as the main color of the jerseys for my fictional Team Yello. Of course, I decided to add yellow as an accenting color due to the team being named Team Yello. I also thought I would add gray into the uniform to make the uniform more complete. I changed the middle pant stripe to gray to provide a change, and also kept the gray outline from the original Detroit Lions to give the 32 a pop.

From there, I removed the background of SoFi Stadium and replaced it with a gradient of black to gray; however, something was missing. Swift seemed to mix into the background, and I needed more contrast to the edit. Therefore, I decided to outline Swift in yellow to bring the contrast, and also to make it seem like Swift was a part of an upper layer. I wanted to add in text graphics of D'Andre on the top left and Swift on the bottom right, but no way I put it seemed to fit nicely. I worked out the kinks (missed elements of Swift's jersey, shirt, and pants) and decided to finish the graphic as you see here.
